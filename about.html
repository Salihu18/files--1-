Our Story

About Us

CyberKonnect Podcast is an educational initiative focused on raising awareness about cybersecurity, digital laws, artificial intelligence risks, and emerging technological threats in Ghana. Through expert discussions and research-based insights, the podcast aims to simplify complex cybersecurity topics for a broad audience.

The Problem We're Solving

Cyber threats in Ghana escalate daily — from misinformation campaigns to unauthorized sharing of intimate images — often catching citizens unaware of the legal repercussions under the Cybersecurity Act 2020.

Recent attacks targeting national infrastructure have inflicted severe economic and personal damages, yet public awareness remains scant. Emerging technologies like AI introduce new vulnerabilities, while career paths in cybersecurity elude many aspiring professionals.

These gaps in knowledge and preparedness are ones our society can ill afford. CyberKonnect Podcast was created to bridge exactly that divide.

What We Aim To Do

Our Objectives

⚖️

Enlighten on Ghana's Cybersecurity Act 2020

Lay bare the criminal nature of acts such as spreading misinformation and the non-consensual distribution of intimate images — empowering citizens to know their rights and responsibilities.

🔎

Examine Major Cyber Incidents

Rigorously analyse at least two major cyber attacks that have struck Ghana, probing their devastating consequences while advancing concrete, forward-looking measures to forestall recurrence.

🤖

Illuminate AI, Emerging Technologies & Careers

Equip listeners with the insight needed to pursue meaningful careers in cybersecurity and contribute toward a more secure digital Ghana in an AI-driven future.
