EP 01

Ghana's Cybersecurity Act 2020: Know Your Digital Rights

A landmark conversation unpacking the legal framework that governs Ghana's digital space. This episode demystifies the law and empowers everyday Ghanaians to understand what is criminal online — and what protections they have.

⏱ Duration

Approximately 40 minutes

📌 Key Topics

  • Overview of the Cybersecurity Act 2020
  • Criminalisation of misinformation online
  • Non-consensual sharing of intimate images
  • Reporting cyber offences in Ghana
  • Audience Q&A with the guest lawyer

EP 02

Under Attack: Major Cyber Incidents in Ghana

An unflinching analysis of at least two significant cyber attacks that have targeted Ghanaian institutions and citizens. Using visual projections and expert analysis, we dissect the devastation caused and chart a clearer path to preventing future breaches.

📊 Format

Panel discussion with visual aids via projector

⏱ Duration

Approximately 40 minutes

📌 Key Topics

  • Case study: National infrastructure attacks
  • Economic and personal damages inflicted
  • How the attacks were carried out
  • Lessons learned and prevention strategies
  • Building Ghana's cyber resilience

🔬 Research Led By

Adjei Ephraim Nhyira & Newton Edward Bernard
